## 核心词汇
-   **图表**：figure, chart, diagram, graph
	- table，line chart，bar chart，pie chart
-   **图画**：illustration, figure, cartoon, drawing, picture, image

## 第一段: 图画/图表描述 (Description)
### 1 图画
#### 1.1 首句：描述
-   **单张图无对比**
	-   As is ==symbolically== depicted in the figure/diagram/drawing above， `+简单句描述环境/基本动作`，passionate/confiden/disgracefult as well as aggressive/optimistic/pessimistic，`+主要动作`

-   **单张图有对比/双图/一图一表**
    1.   As is symbolically depicted in the figure/diagram/drawing above, `+简单句描述环境/基本动作`, in which the actions/attitudes/==mentalities== of the two characters form a very ==ironic and stark== contrast: 
	    -   In the left , `+描述图片 deems/considers/holds`; 
	    -   In the right , `+描述图片`.
    2.   The drawing and the diagram ==briefly portrays== the fact that `+句子总结事实描述`.
	    -   In the left , `+描述图片 deems/considers/holds`; 
	    -   In the right , `+描述图片`.
#### 1.2 主题阐释句
-   **图片有句子**
    -   Below/Above the image, there is a very prominent sentence that roughly says: `+图片下/上面的句子`.

-   **图片无句子**
    1.   The cartoon ==succinctly==/undoubtedly  ==encapsulates== the profound/==dual== essence/nature/==paradox== of `+关键词` and its ==implications==, + `which`
	    - is ==a guiding light== to help us confront a ==myriad== of challenges
	    - attempts to alert the whole society that
	2.   Simple as the drawing is, it conveys a truth that the **utmost importance** ought to be attached to `[核心词]` by the whole society.

### 2 图表
#### 2.1 第一句：总体描述+细节描述
-   **动态图**：
	-   As is ==plainly exhibited== in the diagram/bar chart above，the `主题词 `==witnessed== significant changes from 年份 to 年份
	-   To be specific，`Sth1.` ==climb/rocket/surge== remarkably/==see a remarkble climb== from 数量 to 数量. In the meanwhile, `Sth2.` ==diminish/shrink==/==see a slight increase== from 数量 to 数量 during the same period.
-   **静态图**: 
	-   The bar chart reflects the distribution/composition of `主题词` ==at a glance==, revealing a striking ==disparity/diversity==.
	-   To be specific，the `主题词1、2、3` ==account for/make up==/the proportion of xx% respectively, Especially, `主题词1` ranks the top.
#### 2.2 第二局：过渡句引出+主题阐述
-   It is apparent from the statistics that the landscape of `主题词` has been ==evolving== rapidly in recent years

### 3 文字 : 文字改写+表明态度

## 🏙️ 一、社会方面 (Society)

> [!info]- 核心是发展 (Development)
> - social progress/ development/ stability：社会进步/发展/稳定
> - sustained and healthy growth：持续健康发展
> - high-quality development：高质量发展

> [!example]- 方式是经济 (Economy)
> - rapid economic growth：快速经济增长
> - economic prosperity：经济繁荣
> - rural revitalization：农村振兴
> - expand the market：开拓市场
> - industrial upgrading：产业升级
> - high-end manufacturing：高端制造
> - employment promotion：促进就业
> - promote consumption：促进消费
> - disposable income：可支配收入

> [!tip]- 特征是服务 (Service)
> - public services：公共服务
> - bring convenience to life：生活便利
> - public health security：公共卫生安全
> - infrastructure development：基础设施建设
> - social security system：社保体系
> - elderly care services：养老服务

> [!success]- 目标是和谐 (Harmony)
> - social harmony：社会和谐
> - prosperity of the country：国家富强
> - happiness of the people：人民幸福
> - social cohesion：社会凝聚力
> - social justice：社会公正
> - social morality：社会公德
> - common prosperity：共同富裕

## 🌲 二、自然方面 (Nature)

> [!info]- 核心是持续 (Sustainability)
> - maintain natural ecological balance：维持自然生态平衡
> - sustainable development：可持续发展

> [!example]- 方式是保护 (Protection)
> - improve ecological environment：改善生态环境
> - natural resource preservation：自然资源保护
> - prevent and remedy pollution：预防污染
> - green economy：绿色经济
> - develop renewable energy：发展可再生能源
> - waste classification and recycling：垃圾分类与回收

> [!success]- 目标是和谐 (Harmony)
> - comfortable climate：舒适的气候
> - beautiful natural environment：优美的自然环境
> - peaceful coexistence：和平共处
> - harmony between humanity and nature：人与自然和谐共生

## 🏮 三、文化方面 (Culture)

> [!info]- 核心是传承 (Inheritance)
> - inherit and develop the traditional culture：继承和发扬传统文化
> - cultural protection：文化保护
> - digital preservation：数字化保护

> [!example]- 方式是交流 (Exchange)
> - cultural exchange：文化交流
> - people-to-people exchange：人文交流
> - organize cultural exchange exhibitions：组织文化交流展览
> - enjoy and learn from historical sites：享受和学习历史古迹
> - study/ understand/ respect history：学习/了解/尊重历史

> [!tip]- 态度是包容 (Inclusion)
> - diversity and inclusion：多元化与包容性
> - open and inclusive attitude：开放和包容的态度
> - respect different customs：尊重不同的风俗

> [!success]- 目标是繁荣 (Prosperity)
> - promotion of cultural diversity：促进文化多样性
> - cultural confidence：文化自信
> - cultural identity：文化认同
> - cultural soft power：文化软实力

> - personal growth：个人成长
> - youth development：青年发展
> - the all-round development：全面发展

> [!quote]- 基础是健康 (Health)

> - relieve stress：缓解压力

> - quality of life：生活质量
> - psychological quality：心理素质
> - self-protection awareness：自我保护意识

> [!example]- 方式是学习 (Learning)
> - expand our horizons：开拓眼界
> - broaden our experience：丰富经验
> - acquire knowledge/ skill/ ability：获得知识/技巧/能力
> - give full play to our advantages：发挥自身优势

> - promote one's self-confidence：提升自信
