### 一、 2026 考研英语一 · 终极预测

#### 1. 小作文预测 (Small Composition)
由于 2024 年和 2025 年连续两年考查了**书信（建议/回复信）**，根据“冷门回补”和“题型轮换”原则，今年**通知（Notice）**的概率无限大。

*   **No. 1 预测：通知 (Notice)**
    *   **内容：** 招募志愿者（国际会议、支教、环保活动）、举办讲座/比赛通知。
    *   **理由：** 考研英语一的传统强项，且已隔两年未考。
*   **No. 2 预测：推荐信 (Letter of Recommendation)**
    *   **内容：** 推荐一本书、一部电影、一个旅游景点或中国传统文化。
    *   **理由：** 经典功能性书信，适合套用准备好的素材。
*   **No. 3 预测：邀请信 (Letter of Invitation)**
    *   **内容：** 邀请外教做评委、邀请同学参加聚会。

#### 2. 大作文预测 (Big Composition)
**重磅分析：** PDF 显示 2025 年英语一考查了“表格（Table）”，这是极罕见的情况。根据命题规律，**2026 年极大概率会回归最传统的“图画作文（Drawing/Cartoon）”**，以维持难度和题型的稳定性。

*   **No. 1 核心话题：人工智能与独立思考 (AI & Independent Thinking)**
    *   **场景：** 学生让 AI 写作业 vs 苦读的学生；人们沉溺手机 vs 读书的人。
    *   **寓意：** 科技是双刃剑，不能丧失独立思考能力。
*   **No. 2 核心话题：坚持与行动 (Persistence & Action)**
    *   **场景：** 两个人面对困难，一个空谈一个行动；或者挖井的漫画。
    *   **寓意：** 拒绝焦虑，脚踏实地。
*   **No. 3 核心话题：传统文化与现代融合 (Traditional Culture)**
    *   **场景：** 传统艺术（如京剧、汉服）在现代社会的流行或被忽视。

### 二、大作文实战演练

**预测题目：**
Directions: Write an essay of 160-200 words based on the following drawing. In your essay, you should
1) describe the drawing briefly,
2) interpret its intended meaning, and
3) give your comments.

**设定图画内容：**
两幅图对比。
左图：一个学生躺着睡觉，旁边一个机器人（写着 ChatGPT/AI）在帮他写论文。
右图：一个学生在灯下苦读思考，写得很慢但很认真。
**文字说明：** “省力不等于能力”（Labor-saving is not ability）或 “别让大脑生锈”（Don't let your brain rust）。

**【根据你提供的模版撰写的范文】**

#### **Title: The Paradox of Technology and Intelligence**

**(第一段：图画描述)**
**As is symbolically depicted in the drawing above**, the actions of the two characters form a very **ironic and stark** contrast: **In the left**, a young student is sound asleep while a robot is busy writing his thesis, acting as a "ghostwriter"; **In the right**, another student is burying his head in books, sweating and thinking hard to complete his task independently. **Below the image, there is a very prominent sentence that roughly says:** "Technology makes life easier, but thinking makes us stronger."

**(第二段：原因分析/意义阐述)**
**Not until I see the drawing do I realize the magnitude of** **independent thinking** in this digital era. The fundamental factors that contribute to the above-mentioned tendency may be interpreted as follows. **To begin with**, definitely no one can deny such an obvious fact that **critical thinking** acts as the **foundation upon which** human innovation and social progress are built. **Not only does it** facilitate a deep understanding of knowledge, **but it also** demonstrates the unique value of human wisdom that cannot be simply mimicked by codes. **Furthermore**, the significance of **mental exertion** cannot be **overstated**. It serves as an indispensable instrument for us to cultivate a resilient mind. If we **failed to** cherish the ability to think independently, needless to say, our society **would be confronted** with an unpromising and fruitless future where humans might become slaves to algorithms.

**(第三段：结论与升华)**
**Given the arguments above**, **we can come to the conclusion that** **maintaining independent thinking** is not an option, but a **necessity**. **Under no circumstance should we cease our pursuit of** wisdom and truth, even when technology offers a shortcut. **For one thing**, it is **imperative** for us to take joint efforts to use tools wisely rather than relying on them. **For another**, mass media should **spare no pains** to appeal to young people to balance digital convenience with intellectual development. **By embracing these values**, I am convinced that we will **transcend the passage of time** and embrace a brighter future.

### 三、模版使用小贴士 (Tips)

1.  **第一段的“Left/Right”：** 如果考题是一幅画（比如 2025 年之前的大多数年份），就用模版中的 `Simple as the drawing is...` 或 `As is symbolically depicted in the drawing above...` 直接描述动作，不用写左右对比。
2.  **核心词替换：** 文中的 `[核心词]` (如 **independent thinking**, **critical thinking**) 是得分关键。如果是环保题材，就换成 `environmental protection`；如果是文化题材，就换成 `traditional culture`。
3.  **时态注意：** 描述图画用**一般现在时**或**现在进行时**；中间论述用**一般现在时**。
4.  **字数控制：** 这套模版字数非常充足，大约在 180-200 词之间，非常符合英语一的要求。考试时注意书写工整。
