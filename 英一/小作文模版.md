### 第一部分：核心高分语料库 (万能连接词与高级句型)

在背诵模版前，请掌握这些能瞬间提升作文档次的词汇和句型，它们可以嵌入任何书信或通知中。

#### 1. 逻辑连接词（黄金词）
*   **表示递进/补充：** Moreover (此外), Furthermore (而且), In addition (另外), What is more (更重要的是).
*   **表示转折：** However (然而), Nevertheless (尽管如此), Unfortunately (不幸的是).
*   **表示因果：** Consequently (因此), Due to the fact that... (由于...), As a result (结果).
*   **表示目的：** For the purpose of... (为了...), In order to... (为了).

#### 2. 高级句型（万能套用）
*   **建议句型：** It is advisable/highly recommended for you to... (强烈建议你……)
*   **重要性句型：** ... plays a vital/indispensable role in... (……在……中起着至关重要的作用)
*   **感谢句型：** I would like to convey my heartfelt gratitude to you for... (我想对你……表达我诚挚的谢意)
*   **期待句型：** I am looking forward to your favorable reply at your earliest convenience. (期待您尽早的佳音)
*   **通知开头：** The Student Union is planning to host/organize [Event Name]. (学生会计划举办……)

### 第二部分：2026 考研英语一小作文预测排名与模版

**趋势分析：**
英语一的小作文通常在**书信**（建议、邀请、推荐、道歉）和**告示**（通知）之间轮换。
*   2024、2025 年连续考了书信（建议/回复类）。
*   **因此，2026 年考察「通知（Notice）」的概率极大提升。**
*   同时，「邀请信」和「道歉信」这类很久未考的书信体也是重点防备对象。

#### 预测第一名：通知 (Notice)
**理由：** 自 2023 年以来未考，且是考研英语一的传统强项。特别是**招募志愿者**或**活动通知**。
**参考范文改编：** (结合文件 2 中的“中文演讲比赛”和“英语俱乐部”通知)

**【万能模版：活动/招募通知】**

**Notice**

**Date:** December 20, 2025

**To enhance** the students' awareness of [Topic: e.g., environmental protection/traditional culture] and enrich our campus life, **the Student Union is continuously recruiting volunteers for** the upcoming [Activity Name: e.g., International Cultural Festival]. The details are listed as follows.

**First of all,** the activity is scheduled to be held at [Place] on [Time]. **Secondly,** the main responsibilities of volunteers include [Task 1: e.g., guiding guests] and [Task 2: e.g., maintaining order]. **Last but not least,** candidates who are fluent in spoken English and possess a strong sense of responsibility will be given priority.

Anyone interested in this voluntary work is welcome to send an application to [Email Address] before [Deadline]. Please do not hesitate to join us!

**The Student Union**

#### 预测第二名：邀请信 (Letter of Invitation)
**理由：** 邀请信是极其正式的公务书信，上次考察是 2022 年，符合隔 3-4 年考察一次的频率。
**场景：** 邀请外教/教授做评委、参加毕业典礼或文化活动。

**【万能模版：邀请专家/朋友】**

Dear Professor Smith / [Friend's Name],

**I am writing on behalf of** [Class 3 / the Student Union] **to extend a sincere invitation to you to** attend/participate in the [Event Name], which will be held in [Place] on [Time].

**The primary reason for this invitation is that** you are an expert in this field. We firmly believe that your presence will not only encourage all the students present but also provide professional guidance for us. **Furthermore,** we have arranged a special session for you to share your insights on [Topic].

**We would be extremely honored if you could** accept our invitation. Please let us know your decision at your earliest convenience so that we can make further preparations.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming

---

#### 预测第三名：推荐信 (Letter of Recommendation)
**理由：** 英语一的“王炸”题型，虽然近年考过，但因其可考性极强（推荐书、电影、城市、景点、文化），永远不能掉以轻心。文件 2 中重点预测了推荐景点和名人。
**场景：** 给外国朋友推荐中国传统文化、旅游景点或书籍。

**【万能模版：推荐中国文化/旅游/书籍】**

Dear Paul,

**I am writing this email to recommend** a fascinating [city/book/movie], *[Name of the item]*, to you, which I believe will be of great interest to you.

**The reasons for my recommendation are as follows.** **To begin with,** as a symbol of Chinese traditional culture, it possesses profound historical significance and unique charm. **Moreover,** it is widely acknowledged that [Name] has inspired generations of people, and I am sure you will be impressed by its [unique feature: e.g., breathtaking scenery / deep philosophy]. **It serves as a window for you to** understand the real China.

**I hope you will enjoy it.** If you need further information, please feel free to contact me.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming

#### 预测第四名：道歉信 (Letter of Apology)
**理由：** **超级黑马。** 英语一自 2008 年后再没考过纯粹的道歉信。根据“冷门回补”原则，极有可能突袭。
**场景：** 错过面试、弄丢借的东西、无法赴约。

**【万能模版：因故无法出席/失误】**

Dear [Name],

**I am writing to express my sincerest apologies for** [not being able to attend the meeting / losing the book you lent me]. **I am truly sorry for** any inconvenience this may have caused you.

**The reason is that** [Reason: e.g., I suddenly fell ill / I was caught in an unexpected emergency] yesterday. **To make up for my fault,** I would like to [Solution: e.g., reschedule the appointment / buy a new copy for you] at your convenience. I promise that such a mistake will never occur again.

**Once again, please accept my deepest apologies.** I am looking forward to your forgiveness.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming

---

#### 预测第五名：会议纪要 (Minutes)
**理由：** 文件 2 中王江涛预测的第 10 篇是会议纪要。虽然这通常是英语二的考点，但大纲中英语一也有此要求。如果考出来会是“大杀器”，因为大部分人没背格式。只需记住格式即可防身。

**【万能格式模版】**

**Minutes of the Meeting on [Meeting Topic]**

**Time:** 2:00 p.m. - 4:00 p.m., December 20, 2025
**Place:** The Conference Room, Library
**Present:** All members of the Student Union
**Presided by:** Li Ming

**Summary of the meeting:**
The meeting aimed to discuss [Topic: e.g., the plan for the Charity Sale]. **Decisions were made as follows:**
1.  **Firstly,** it was agreed that the event will take place next Friday to ensure maximum participation.
2.  **Secondly,** Wang Hua was assigned to be responsible for the promotion and poster design.
3.  **Finally,** all the funds raised will be donated to Project Hope to help children in rural areas.

**Submitted by:** Li Ming

### 考场策略总结 (Exam Strategy)

1.  **审题第一：** 拿到卷子先看是 **Letter (书信)** 还是 **Notice (通知)**。
    *   如果是 Notice，一定要写标题（居中），日期（右上角），落款（右下角）。
    *   如果是 Letter，注意称呼和落款格式。
2.  **不要使用真名：** 永远使用 "Li Ming" 或 "The Student Union"（除非题目另有规定），绝对不要签自己的名字。
3.  **字数控制：** 题目要求 100 词左右，建议写 **100-120 词**。不要写太长，容易出错且浪费时间。
4.  **卷面整洁：** 哪怕字写得不好看，也要工整，不要涂改。

祝你在 12 月 20 日的考试中，小作文拿下高分！
